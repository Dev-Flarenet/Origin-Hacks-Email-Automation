ABOUT ORIGIN HACKS

OriginHacks is a national-level hackathon bringing together innovators, developers, and problem solvers to build real-time solutions for industry and societal challenges. The journey begins with online registration and team formation, followed by an idea submission and abstract evaluation round for shortlisted teams. Selected participants then enter a high-intensity, 24-hour live development phase where they transform concepts into functional projects.
Throughout the hacking period, teams receive professional guidance from mentors to refine their technical and strategic approach. The event culminates in a demo session where final projects are presented to an expert judging panel, concluding with a valedictory and prize distribution ceremony to recognize the most impactful solutions.
